How long it takes to design?

For a single-page design, the process typically takes 5-7 days. For a 5-8 page website, expect the development time to range from 14-24 days.

+

How do I associate with IT Company?

The journey begins when you reach out with your requirements. We will review your needs, and based on our discussion, we will help you choose the engagement model that best fits your project. Once you've decided, we will begin the development process.

How can I contact you?

Can you help make my current website more professional?

Absolutely! We offer both custom website design from scratch and enhancement services for existing websites, helping you achieve a more polished and professional look.

+

When is payment due?

After our initial discussion and once you decide to proceed with our services, we require a 50% deposit of the total development cost. Upon receiving your payment, we will issue an official receipt, and work on your website will begin immediately. Once the website is complete, we will present it to you. You will have 5 days for revisions. If you're satisfied with the design, we will consider the project complete and collect the remaining 50% balance.

+

Are there any monthly fees?

Monthly fees depend on the package you choose. We also offer affordable monthly retainer options that include free maintenance services.

+

What will I receive after the website is completed?

Once your website is fully developed, you'll receive a tailored website that meets your business needs, along with the source codes as part of your purchase.
